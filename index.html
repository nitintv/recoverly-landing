The Problem

By the time you find out a patient dropped off, it's already too late.

Silent drop-offs

A patient gets approved for Xtandi and never picks it up. Your coordinator finds out three weeks later when she pulls the chart for something else and sees the fill never went through. Nobody flagged it. Nobody knew.

Manual worklists

Your MAs and coordinators are buried in spreadsheets, trying to remember who to call. When someone is out sick or quits, their list disappears with them. The new person starts from scratch.

Staff burnout

Your care team stays late, takes work home, and worries about the patients they couldn't get to today. They're managing 200 patients with a spreadsheet and their own memory. That's not a system — it's unsustainable.
